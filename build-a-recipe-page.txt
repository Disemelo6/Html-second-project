    <h1>Chocolate Muffins Recipe</h1>
    <p>Delicious Oven Baked Chocolate Muffins Recipe For Your Family And Guests</p>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1kg Sifted Self Raising Flour</li>
      <li>500g Melted Margarine</li>
      <li>24 Slabs Chocolate Melted </li>
      <li>100g Sugar</li>
      <li>1 Table Spoon Salt</li>
      <li>500ml Milk</li>
      <li>100g Chocolate Powder</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>In A Large Bowl Mix All Dry Ingredients </li>
      <li>Then In Another Bowl Mix Margarine, Milk And Melted Chocolate </li>
      <li>Take The Margarine Mixture And Add To The Dry Mixture,Mix With A Large Wooden Spoon Until Consistent</li>
      <li>Grease The Muffins Pans,Then Pour Your Mixture Inside The Pans</li>
        <li>Bake For 30 Minutes On A 180g Oven,Enjoy With Your Favorite DrinküßÅ</> 
    </ol>
